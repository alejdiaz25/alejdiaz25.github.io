I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at UT Austin (graduating December 2026) with a Minor in Entrepreneurship, building hardware systems where tight tolerances and high stakes are the standard.

My work spans defense manufacturing at Northrop Grumman — developing Python ML pipelines for F-35 metrology data and contributing to next-generation LLM architectures for restricted programs — to leading structural design on liquid bi-propellant rockets at TREL and redesigning unsprung assemblies for Longhorn Racing Electric.

I'm drawn to problems where the physics is hard, the tolerances are tight, and the stakes are real.
